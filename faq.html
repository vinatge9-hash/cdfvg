Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to common questions about buying, selling, and our services.

Do you provide real estate IDX feeds?

Yes. Everest Real Estate integrates IDX/MLS feeds to display real-time listings, ensuring clients see current opportunities. We securely connect to multiple MLS providers to keep data fresh while honoring licensing agreements.

Can I search by neighborhood or school district?

Absolutely. Our search supports location radius, neighborhood filters, school districts, and other criteria to help you find the right fit.

Is there a mortgage calculator available?

Yes. Use our mortgage calculator on property pages or the dedicated mortgage tool page to estimate monthly payments based on price, down payment, and interest rate.
